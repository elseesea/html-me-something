Avoiding Phishing

Cyber criminals steal information and money at an alarming frequency. Most often, the first step in their process is to get a user to fall for a phish.

A phish is a communication designed to get the user to take some action that would enable the cyber criminal to move to the next step in the process.

The most common type of phish is sent via email. And usually, the email asks the user to click a link or open an attachment
